# Customer Intelligence Platform

---

## Project Summary

The Customer Intelligence Platform is an end-to-end customer analytics project developed using Google BigQuery, SQL, and Tableau Public. The project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, customer segmentation, and business KPIs to support data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations collect vast amounts of customer transaction data but often lack meaningful insights into customer behavior and purchasing patterns. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence through SQL analysis and interactive dashboards.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-value customers
- Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Monitor monthly revenue trends
- Compare revenue across product categories
- Analyze payment method preferences
- Build an executive customer intelligence dashboard
